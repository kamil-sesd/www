About me
I specialize in the automation of data processing, advanced analysis and reporting. I have been professionally involved in data analysis since 2014. I have worked for companies in the research, cybersecurity, HR analytics and also in public institutions.
The tools I use on a daily basis are R, Shiny, HTML, CSS, JavaScript. I find the most satisfaction in projects in which results are reported in the form of applications/interactive dashboards. These are tailored Business Intelligence solutions.
What sets me apart is my commitment, reliability, enthusiasm and ability to recognize needs. If this description interests you, check out my offer and sample applications that I have posted in the portfolio.

What data tells about my blog?
The blog is a try to build something that I like to read (browse), and there is definitely not enough of it on the Polish Internet. A relatively loose format in which data is an excuse to draw attention to a topic, which is missed by public eye and which is, to use language former coach of the Polish football team, extremely important.
I do not intend to create dissertations, my goal is not even to provide an comprehensive description of the changes taking place in society. I would like only to present a handful of interesting information and synthesize it. Based on publicly available data. Without complicated conceptual framework and methodological intricacies, but in a way that is understandable to everyone and attractive to the eye.
Posts will have different forms. Sometimes this will be a videoblog, other times it will be an analytical dashboard with a description. I intend to play with form and conventions. Will I succeed? Time will tell :)
